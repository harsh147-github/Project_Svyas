The challenge today — for both sides
🏛 Decision-Makers Face

Thousands of unstructured inputs from CPGRAMS, Twitter, RTI, media, and party workers — with no way to prioritize, no synthesized solutions, and no data on what citizens actually need most. Decisions made on incomplete information. Crises detected too late.

👤 Citizens Face

No structured way to contribute solutions — only complaint boxes. No visibility into follow-up or action. No collective voice between elections. Engagement drops. Civic participation stays low between election cycles.

Sushasan bridges this gap — giving citizens a zero-friction way to contribute solutions, and giving decision-makers a real-time AI-powered intelligence dashboard with prioritized problems, ready action plans, and public support data.

Existing apps that fell short

PMC Road Mitra (Pune) — launched Aug 2025, ceased working within 6 months. CPGRAMS — 62% satisfaction rate. MyGov.in — top-down surveys, no citizen-initiated problem solving. These taught us what NOT to build.

What Sushasan does differently

AI synthesis (not just complaint collection). Governance hierarchy routing (not one inbox). Public dashboard for transparency AND official recognition. Distribution via WhatsApp groups (not a new app to download).
02 — How It Works

Citizens contribute. AI synthesizes. Government implements.

A simple flow that respects everyone's time — citizens contribute in 5 seconds, AI does the heavy lifting, and decision-makers receive ready-to-implement intelligence.

Citizens speak in any language — zero friction

Type or speak a "vibe answer" in Hindi, Marathi, English — any language. "Kharadi ke raaste bikul kharab hain." No formal writing needed. Voice, text, or image. 5 seconds.

AI refines input into structured intelligence

AI converts casual input into: Problem classification, governance level, proposed solution, feasibility estimate. Citizen sees what AI understood — can approve or correct it.

AI synthesizes thousands of inputs into optimal solutions

Thousands of citizen opinions become ONE synthesized solution — weighted themes, action items, responsible department, timeline, cost estimate, and confidence score.

Problems are interconnected and decomposed

"Hinjewadi traffic" = 4 sub-problems across 4 departments: PMPML (buses), PWD (roads), PMRDA (metro), companies (parking). Each gets assigned to the right authority, solvable in parallel.

Decision-makers receive prioritized intelligence

Officials see a ranked dashboard with AI-synthesized solutions, cost estimates, urgency scores, and one-click response. Responding is easier than any existing channel — and gives public credit.

Transparent tracking — everyone sees progress

Citizens see whether authorities have responded. Officials get public recognition for swift action. Auto-escalation when 5+ areas report the same issue. Governance becomes a collaborative conversation.

Built on India's governance hierarchy

Every level — from housing society to Parliament — has its own dashboard. Problems auto-escalate when they're systemic.

03 — The AI Engine

Why AI makes this possible at national scale

Without AI, citizen engagement platforms collect complaints. With AI, Sushasan synthesizes collective intelligence. Six autonomous jobs power the platform.

🗣️
Lowers the bar

"paani nahi aa raha" → structured intelligence. Any language. 5 seconds.

🧠
Synthesizes at scale

10,000 inputs → ONE solution with themes, action items, costs. 15 seconds.

🎯
Routes to the right desk

Pothole → ward PWD. Water policy → state. Railway → Ministry of Railways.

JOB 1 — REFINE<3 sec

Converts casual citizen input into structured intelligence. Auto-detects language. Citizen approves or corrects the AI interpretation.

JOB 2 — ROUTE<2 sec

Auto-classifies governance level and department. Problems reach the right desk immediately, not after weeks of transfers.

JOB 3 — SYNTHESIZE5-15 sec

Aggregates inputs into optimal solutions. Themes, action items, responsible authority, timeline, cost estimates, and confidence scores.

JOB 4 — BUBBLE-UPevery 6 hrs

5+ wards report same issue → city-level synthesis. Early intelligence for decision-makers. Systemic issues surfaced proactively.

JOB 5 — INTERCONNECTon synthesis

Maps causal relationships between problems. Decomposes compound issues into parallel workstreams for multi-department coordination.

JOB 6 — PETITION5-10 sec

Generates formal documentation from synthesis. Official format with citizen data, AI recommendations, and implementation roadmap.

06 — Government Integration

Designed for India's governance ecosystem

Sushasan aligns with active Central Government priorities, integrates with existing systems, and is deployable on Indian government infrastructure. Open-source. Full data sovereignty.

Digital India Mission

Extends Digital India from service delivery (DigiLocker, UMANG) to citizen participation — the next frontier. Citizens don't just receive services; they contribute intelligence.

CPGRAMS Enhancement

Pre-processing intelligence layer for CPGRAMS. Instead of raw complaints, Sushasan feeds structured, AI-synthesized solutions with cost estimates into existing grievance workflows.

Smart Cities Mission

Citizen intelligence layer for 100 Smart City Command Centers. Combine sensor data with citizen data for comprehensive governance intelligence.

Aspirational Districts (NITI Aayog)

Real-time citizen intelligence for 112 focus districts. What's working, what needs attention — synthesized and prioritized for Collectors.

BHASHINI Integration

Full multilingual support via BHASHINI for all 22 scheduled languages. Every Indian contributes in their mother tongue. AI handles the rest.

Data Sovereignty & Security

All data on Indian servers (NIC/MeghRaj compatible). Open-source, fully auditable. Compliant with DPDP Act 2023. No vendor lock-in. Zero foreign data transfer.

For different government stakeholders
For NITI Aayog / PMO

"Sushasan creates the citizen intelligence layer India doesn't have yet. Synthesized, prioritized, and routed to the right authority — a national pulse monitor that strengthens governance at every level."

For Municipal Commissioners

"Your top 10 citizen priorities — pre-synthesized with solutions, cost estimates, and the responsible department. Respond in one click. Get public recognition for responsiveness."

For MeitY / NIC

"Open-source. PostgreSQL + Node.js. Deployable on NIC or MeghRaj. AI layer switchable to BHASHINI + Indian LLMs. No vendor lock-in. Full government auditability."

🏛 Deployment Options

Option A: NIC infrastructure (full government control). Option B: MeghRaj GI Cloud. Option C: Hybrid — CDN frontend, Gov Cloud backend, AI API with Indian fallback.

💰 Cost to Run

MVP: ₹2,000-3,000/month runs a city. Open-source = no licensing fees ever. At national scale on Gov Cloud, costs scale linearly with usage, not infrastructure.
