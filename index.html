The Challenge

Intelligence at Scale

Every single day, 1.4 billion citizens generate insights, solutions, and community intelligence that rarely reach the right decision-makers in a structured format. Emails overflow. Social media creates noise. Valuable ideas from citizens are lost in the sheer volume of unstructured data.

What if we could harness the collective intelligence of an entire nation? What if every voice could be heard, synthesized, structured, and securely routed to the exact authority handling that domain? That's Sushasan.

Our Process

How Sushasan Works

01

Collect Inputs Naturally

Citizens contribute hyper-local issues through voice, text, or images in any language. Inputs are logged, structured, and time-stamped instantly.

02

AI Synthesizes Patterns

Advanced neural models (GPT-4o) identify themes, detect systemic consensus, filter noise, and merge thousands of duplicate inputs into one optimal solution.

03

Route Intelligently

Action plans and cost estimates are automatically classified and securely routed to the relevant municipal, state, or central authority dashboard.

04

Dual Interface Outcomes

Decision-makers get a clean, prioritized data queue. Citizens get complete transparency on input processing, attribution, and resolution statuses.

Under the Hood

The AI Engine

Refine & Route

GPT-4o-mini (< 3s Latency)

Converts casual vibe input in any language into structured intelligence. Instantly classifies the governance level (Ward, City, State, National) and routes the problem to the exact right dashboard.

Synthesize

GPT-4o (5-15s Latency)

Takes 10,000 parallel inputs about the same issue and merges them into a single, highly actionable optimal solution featuring root causes, action items, cost estimates, and ranked citizen consensus.

Interconnect

GPT-4o (5s Latency)

Identifies causal relationships between problems across locations. Maps compound systemic crises across overlapping departments for parallel resolution.

Bubble-Up Escalation

Async Polling Engine

Constant systemic pattern detection. If 5+ dependent locations (Wards) report identical problems, the engine instantly auto-escalates the synthesized brief to the parent location (City Commissioner).
